Coming Soon!

Mesmerize Games and Gato D proudly present Binary Realms Data.Warriors, a new free and open-source strategy card game you can print and assemble yourself to play. Right now we're just finishing up the prototyping phase and in a short while we'll be releasing all files to the public!

With this game, you'll be able to print and build decks using freely available cards and ruleset, and play with your friends in intense virtual battles. In the fantasy of the game's lore and themes, it's about hacking each other using executing commands, loading programs and daemons into your memory and use them to win against your opponents and their code.

In case you want us to keep you updated with fresh news and updates about this game, please consider subscribing to our mailing list.
